This structured plan provides a compelling overview of Navgurukul, highlighting its origins, vision, and goals in providing quality education and opportunities in the tech industry.

History

Navgurukul was founded in 2016 by Abhishek Gupta and Rishabh Verma with a vision to provide quality education to underprivileged youth in India. Starting with a small group of students in a rented apartment, Navgurukul aimed to offer an alternative to traditional education by providing free, residential, and skill-based training in computer programming and software development. Over the years, Navgurukul has grown into a renowned institution, empowering hundreds of students from marginalized communities with the skills and opportunities to pursue successful careers in the tech industry.

Vision

Navgurukul envisions a future where every young person in India has access to quality education and economic opportunities, regardless of their socio-economic background. We aspire to create a world where talent is recognized and nurtured, where innovation thrives, and where every individual has the chance to fulfill their potential. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide and empower the next generation of leaders and changemakers who will drive India's growth and development in the 21st century.

Goals

  • Empowerment Through Education: Our primary goal at Navgurukul is to empower underprivileged youth with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the digital economy, breaking the cycle of poverty and inequality.
  • Skill Development: We aim to provide hands-on, industry-relevant training in computer programming and software development, equipping our students with the technical skills and confidence to excel in the tech industry.
  • Employment Opportunities: Our goal is to connect our graduates with meaningful employment opportunities in the technology sector, facilitating their transition into the workforce and supporting their long-term career growth and success.
  • Community Building: We strive to build a strong and supportive community of alumni, mentors, and partners who share our vision and values, providing ongoing support, guidance, and networking opportunities for our students and graduates.
  • Social Impact: We are committed to making a positive impact on society by promoting education, diversity, and inclusion, advocating for equal access to opportunities for all, and contributing to the development of a more equitable and sustainable future for India.
