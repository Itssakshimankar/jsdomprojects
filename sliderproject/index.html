Gothic Architecture

Gothic architecture features pointed arches, ribbed vaults, and flying buttresses, creating majestic and ornate structures.

Baroque Architecture

Baroque architecture is known for its grandeur, bold forms, and lavish ornamentation, reflecting the power of the era.

Modernist Architecture

Modernist architecture embraces simplicity, clean lines, and functional design, revolutionizing 20th-century building practices.

Postmodern Architecture

Postmodern architecture combines eclectic styles and historical references, offering a playful and ironic approach to design.

Futuristic Architecture

Futuristic architecture envisions bold and innovative designs, incorporating cutting-edge technologies and sustainable practices.
